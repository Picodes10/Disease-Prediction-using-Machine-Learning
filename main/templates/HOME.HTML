About the Project

This project leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to predict various human diseases based on symptoms and health data. By analyzing patient information, our model provides early warnings and aids in better decision-making for healthcare professionals.

Features

  • Simple and user-friendly interface
  • Fast and accurate results
  • Supports multiple diseases
  • Early detection for better treatment
  • Secure and privacy-focused

About Us

We are a team of machine learning enthusiasts dedicated to developing predictive models for human diseases. Our goal is to leverage AI and data science to assist in early diagnosis and medical research.
